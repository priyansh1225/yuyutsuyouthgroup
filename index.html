Who We Are

About Us

Yuyutsu Youth Group is built on compassion, action, discipline, and teamwork. We focus on creating practical impact through awareness, animal care, public service, and community participation.

Our Mission

To encourage youth to take meaningful action and build a kinder, cleaner, stronger community through consistent service.

Our Focus

Animal feeding, social awareness, community help, cleanliness efforts, and helping where even small work creates visible change.

Why We Started

We did not wait for perfect resources. We started with purpose, effort, and belief that real impact begins with small steps.
